About us

LinkStudent is a website that connect Birzeit Students with companies in which they are able to take a training with them. We make sure that the companies will connect with best BZU students.

Services

Companies

  • Post training positions
  • search for training students

Students

  • students can search for companies to train in them.
  • They can post their CV

instructors

  • The instructors can recommend students for a training position
  • They can ask companies to open training positions for students
